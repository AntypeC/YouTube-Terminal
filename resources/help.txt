

Help Menu:

Commands:
    home            - View the home screen
    search <keyword>- Search for a video
    select <number> - Select a video from search results
    play            - Play the currently selected video
    pause           - Pause the currently playing video
    stop            - Stop the currently playing video
    volume <0-10>   - Adjust the video volume
    comment <text>  - Comment on the currently selected video
    quit            - Exit the terminal YouTube app
